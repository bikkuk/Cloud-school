# Lesson 1: What Is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is software that can recognize patterns and make useful predictions.

## Simple idea
- AI learns from examples.
- AI does **not** think like a human.
- AI can still make mistakes.

## Everyday examples
- Voice assistants
- Spam filters in email
- Captions for photos
- Suggestions in maps

## Key safety reminder
Always double-check important AI answers with trusted people in your life.
