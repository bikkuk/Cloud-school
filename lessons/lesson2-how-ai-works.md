# Lesson 2: How AI Answers Questions

An AI model predicts the next word based on patterns from training data.

## What this means for you
- AI can sound confident even when wrong.
- Short, clear questions usually work better.
- Asking follow-up questions can improve answers.

## Good question format
1. State your topic.
2. Ask for simple language.
3. Ask for steps or examples.

## Key safety reminder
If an answer seems surprising, stop and ask for clarification.
