# Lesson 4: AI-Related Scams

Scammers can use AI to create realistic messages and voices.

## Warning signs
- Urgent pressure: “Act now!”
- Requests for gift cards or wire transfers
- Unknown caller asks for private details
- Message has odd grammar or unusual links

## Safe response plan
1. Pause.
2. Verify with a trusted contact using a known phone number.
3. Never send money based on one message.

## Key safety reminder
When in doubt, wait and verify.
